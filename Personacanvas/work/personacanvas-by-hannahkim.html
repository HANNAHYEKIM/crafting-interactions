Project Overview

‘PersonaCanvas’ suggests the new digital art experience by not only showcasing stunning AI-generated art but also by creating a personalized journey for each user. With seamless integration between our mobile app and the digital frame, users can immerse themselves in a curated collection of unique artwork that speaks to their individuality.
